Seoul Share Bike Reallocation System Optimization Strategy
Growth Hackers
Purpose and Reason for the Analysis
  • Inefficient way of re-allocating bikes resulted in huge costs of Seoul Metropolitan Government's budget
  • Through machine learning we tried to find more optimal way of re-allocating the share bike
  • Find a way to make customers to be involved into re-allocation system in order to alleviate the costs








Target Specification
  • Selected time of a day that the imbalance of number of bikes in each stations maximizes
  • Selected efficient area that the re-allocation of bikes should be happened








Finding Degree of Need in Reallocation
  • Chose the threshold for selecting stations that need more bikes and stations that have more than it needs
  • Explanation of the variables in the data we will be using for our model
  • Suggested XGBoost for predicting the number of returning bikes as well as the number of bikes that will be taken














Reallocation Credit Calculation
  • Method of making customers to return bikes to specific station through providing credit was suggested
  • Development of credit calculation was formulated




Conclusion
  • Demonstrated the re-allocation system through providing credits to users
