    • Difficulties I Faced During Work

  • In the analysis process, the purpose and direction of the analysis are not clear, so the analysis cannot be directed and the analysis is spent a lot of time on unnecessary parts.
  • The analysis was made in a way that the figures should be concentrated on, and that is why they are different, not in this respect and what the results are.

  • As the analysis was conducted without a deep understanding of each figure, it was difficult to quickly understand the relationship between contents by comparing the previous given figures.
  • It was difficult to determine what was the most appropriate number to verify the hypothesis that they wanted to verify, and to come up with better numbers.

  • The resulting figures were first viewed in the content and lacked understanding of the content or format of the individual content.
  • Lack of understanding of content prevented the creation of a proper environment when creating a comparison between content.
  • Comparison between groups of content mixed with inappropriate content, or analysis without the type of content to be included, required to be re-analyzed from scratch;

  • This is the part where I thought the most about it, but it didn't work out at all.
  • Hyundai Card issues a lot of content compared to other competitors, but there is not enough content to subdivide various types of content into homogeneous groups.
  • The biggest factor that will affect individual content is the content itself, the form of publication, and advertising costs.
  • Advertising costs try to look at figures that exclude the influence of advertising costs to some extent through Cost-related figures, but this does not take into account the increase in efficiency due to the increase in advertising costs.
  • It is not difficult to divide according to the type of publication, but when compared accordingly, the performance of the contents of individual contents is compared, which is not appropriate for comparison of higher groups (ex. New Retro VS Non-Newlet).
  • It was difficult to determine how evaluation of two arbitrary groups could be done beyond evaluation of individual content.

  • As working-level officials were very sensitive to the contents of the report, they felt that apart from the actual analysis, they had to reorganize the analysis results according to the format and direction that Hyundai Card wanted.
