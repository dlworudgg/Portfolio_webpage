Difficulties I Faced During Work

  • In the analysis process, the purpose and direction of the analysis were not clear, the analysis spent a lot of time on unnecessary parts.
  • The analysis should be conducted under specific hypothesis rather than going through numbers looking for insights to pop up.

  • As the analysis was conducted without a deep understanding of each figure, it was difficult to quickly understand the relationship between contents by comparing the previous given figures.
  • It was difficult to determine which figure was the most appropriate figure for evaluation for the hypothesis that we wanted to verify.

  • I tried to analyze the figures before understanding the individual content.
  • Lack of understanding in contents prevented more appropriate comparison between contents.
  • It took more time to study on the contents and re-analyze from the scratch

  • One of our clients issued a lot of contents compared to other competitors, but there is not enough contents to subdivide into homogeneous groups.
  • The biggest factor that affects individual content is the content itself, the form of publication, and the advertising costs.
  • It was difficult to determine how evaluation of two arbitrary groups could be done beyond evaluation of individual content.

  • As working-level officials were very sensitive to the report, they felt that apart from the actual analysis, they had to reorganize the analysis results according to the format and direction that they wanted.
